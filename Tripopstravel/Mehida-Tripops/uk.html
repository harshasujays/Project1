United Kingdom

Historic Castles, Dramatic Coastlines & Cultural Charm

1. Overview

The United Kingdom (UK) is a blend of history, royal heritage, scenic countryside, and vibrant modern cities. It includes England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland.

2. Location & Geography

Located in northwestern Europe, the UK is an island nation surrounded by the Atlantic Ocean and North Sea. It features rolling hills, rugged coastlines, lakes, and mountains.

3. Adventure Activities

🏞️ Hiking in Scottish Highlands
🚴 Cycling in Countryside
🏰 Castle Exploring
🌊 Coastal Cliff Walks
🏄 Surfing in Cornwall

4. Famous Tourist & Adventure Locations

  • London – Capital city
    Home to Big Ben, Tower Bridge & Buckingham Palace.
  • Edinburgh – Historic charm
    Famous for Edinburgh Castle and scenic Arthur’s Seat.
  • Lake District – Natural beauty
    Perfect for hiking, boating & peaceful landscapes.
  • Stonehenge – Ancient wonder
    One of the world’s most mysterious prehistoric sites.
  • Giant’s Causeway – Natural formation
    Unique basalt columns along the Northern Ireland coast.

5. Best Time to Visit

May to September offers pleasant weather. Spring and summer are ideal for countryside and city exploration.

6. Estimated Budget

Daily expenses range from ₹9,000 to ₹20,000 depending on city and accommodation type.
